Roses

    Roses come in a variety of colors along with different meanings. There are approximately 150 wild species of roses and 30,000 cultivated varieties. From love all the way to hate there is a rose for any occasion. The beauty of the rose stays the same. Most would love the red roses the red is eye catching and aloring with a universal meaning of love and passion.
